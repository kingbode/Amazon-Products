Demo for the tool that gets the following from Amazon website:

1- Product Profile
2- Product Reviews
3- Product Questions and answers from clients

all you need is Product ASIN number : B07MW4BR8D

Amazon_Asin_V2.1.exe -a B07MW4BR8D -r 20 -q 20

command line to collect the reviews and questions 20 page for each + product profile

data will be collected in ASINS folder
